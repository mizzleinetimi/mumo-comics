# Streaming Dreams

## Panel 1

![Mumo staring at multiple streaming app icons](/comics/streaming-dreams/panel-01.svg)

**Mumo**: "Okay, I want to watch 'Space Rangers.' Which app is it on?"

**Phone**: "Searching... Found on: StreamFlix, WatchNow, BingeBox, and ViewHub."

## Panel 2

![Mumo looking confused](/comics/streaming-dreams/panel-02.svg)

**Mumo**: "Wait, it's on FOUR different services? How is that possible?"

**Friend**: "Different regions, different licensing deals. Welcome to the streaming wars!"

## Panel 3

![Mumo with a notebook making calculations](/comics/streaming-dreams/panel-03.svg)

**Mumo**: "Let me calculate... If I subscribe to all of them, that's $60 a month!"

**Friend**: "That's more than cable used to cost."

**Mumo**: "We've come full circle!"

## Panel 4

![Mumo sharing an account](/comics/streaming-dreams/panel-04.svg)

**Mumo**: "Solution: I'll share accounts with friends!"

**Friend**: "That's the spirit! Just don't tell the streaming services."

## Panel 5

![Mumo finally watching the show](/comics/streaming-dreams/panel-05.svg)

**Mumo**: "Finally watching! This is great!"

**TV**: "Your free trial has ended. Please subscribe to continue."

**Mumo**: "NOOOOO!"
